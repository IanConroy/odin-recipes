How to Make Pizza I Never Even Tried!

I haven't tried this, but it probably works

Ingredients needed
  • Cheese
  • Crust
  • Pepperoni
  • Red sauce
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Spread sauce on the crust evenly
  3. Spread cheese evenly across in a similar manner
  4. Distribute pepperonis evenly as well
  5. Once oven reaches 425 degrees, place the pizza in the oven
  6. Take it out after approximately 15-20 minutes
  7. Mmmm, pizza! Delicious!
