Collard Greens

A delicious collard green recipe

An innovative combination of ingredients you've never seen before that sends you to heaven.

Ingredients
  • Collard Greens
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Goya Adobo
  • Balsamic Vinegar
Recipe Instructions
  1. Chop the collard greens to your preferred size
  2. Mince garlic if not already done
  3. Dice the onion
  4. Pour olive oil onto a frying pan on medium heat
  5. Combine collard greens and garlic
  6. Turn the collard greens over until coated in olive oil
  7. Add chopped onions
  8. Turn the heat down one and a half markers
  9. Pour adobo on collard greens
  10. Turn collard greens again, stirring around a bit more
  11. Lower the heat all the way to the lowest setting
  12. Add adobo again
  13. After approximately five minutes, add balsamic vinegar
  14. Turn and stir the ingredients again, adding adobo one more time
  15. Turn off the heat completely and serve! Enjoy!
